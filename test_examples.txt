Malicious Text 4:
"PayPal Security Alert: Unusual activity detected on your account. Your account has been temporarily suspended. Verify your identity immediately: https://paypal-verify-security.com/login?alert=urgent or your account will be permanently closed."

Malicious Text 5:
"Microsoft Tech Support: Your computer has been infected with a virus! Immediate action required. Call 1-800-XXX-XXXX now or click: http://microsoft-support-urgent.com/fix-virus to remove the threat before your data is stolen."

Malicious Text 6:
"Bank Security Alert: Suspicious transaction detected on your credit card ending in 1234. Verify this transaction immediately: https://bank-verify-transaction.com/secure?card=1234 or your card will be blocked permanently."

Malicious Text 7:
"Amazon Order Issue: Your recent order cannot be processed due to payment failure. Update your payment method immediately: http://amazon-payment-update.com/urgent?order=ABC123 or your order will be cancelled."

Malicious Text 8:
"Social Security Administration: Your benefits have been suspended due to suspicious activity. Verify your identity now: https://ssa-verify-benefits.com/secure?ssn=XXX-XX-XXXX to restore your benefits."
